Career Paths & Growth

Careers That Grow With You

At K&A, your career is a ladder, not a lane. Engineers move into project management. Designers shift into digital services. Graduates grow into project leads, and many of our senior leaders started out as entry-level engineers or architects.

Our scale (30+ offices, 6,500+ professionals, multiple disciplines) means your next step rarely requires leaving. Move across offices, across geographies, or into new service lines.
