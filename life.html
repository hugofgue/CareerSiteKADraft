Life at Khatib & Alami

Where people, purpose, and place come together

A Workplace Shaped by 60+ Years of Experience

Since 1959, Khatib & Alami has brought together architects, engineers, planners, and innovators to shape the skylines, infrastructure, and communities of the Middle East and beyond. Our team of 6,500+ professionals works across 30+ offices and 25+ nationalities — building not just iconic projects, but a workplace where people grow, belong, and do their best work.
